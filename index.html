Who is Jagannath? Hvem er Jagannath?

Lord Jagannath — "Jagat" (universe) + "Natha" (Lord) — is the all-merciful form of Lord Krishna worshipped for thousands of years in Puri, Odisha. His distinctive form, with large compassionate eyes that see every soul equally, represents Krishna in His most accessible, non-exclusive aspect. He stands with His elder brother Baladeva and beloved sister Subhadra, welcoming all who come — regardless of background, belief, or origin. Herre Jagannath — "Jagat" (univers) + "Natha" (Herre) — er den altbarmhjertige form af Herre Krishna, tilbedt i tusinder af år i Puri, Odisha. Hans karakteristiske form med store medfølende øjne, der ser alle sjæle ens, repræsenterer Krishna i Hans mest tilgængelige aspekt. Han står med sin storebror Baladeva og elskede søster Subhadra og byder alle velkommen.

"The Lord of the universe accepts everyone. There is no restriction. This is the special mercy of Lord Jagannath." "Universets Herre accepterer alle. Der er ingen begrænsning. Dette er den særlige nåde fra Herre Jagannath." — Srila Prabhupada

Ratha-Yatra

The Festival of ChariotsVognfestivalen — Ratha-Yatra

Once a year, Lord Jagannath, Baladeva and Subhadra ride magnificent chariots through the streets of Copenhagen — an ancient tradition stretching back thousands of years to Puri, India. Everyone who pulls the ropes or witnesses the procession receives the Lord's blessing. En gang om året rider Herre Jagannath, Baladeva og Subhadra på prægtige vogne gennem Københavns gader — en ældgammel tradition fra Puri, Indien. Alle der trækker tovene eller overværer processionen modtager Herrens velsignelse.
