The Three Lords De tre Herrer

Who is Jagannath? Hvem er Jagannath?

Lord Jagannath — "Jagat" (universe) + "Natha" (Lord) — is the all-merciful form of Lord Krishna, worshipped since ancient times in Puri, Odisha. His distinctive form, with large round eyes that see all, represents the absolute totality of existence. He stands with His brother Baladeva and sister Subhadra, blessing all who come to Him regardless of caste, creed, or origin. Herre Jagannath — "Jagat" (univers) + "Natha" (Herre) — er den altbarmhjertige form af Herre Krishna, der er blevet tilbedt siden oldtiden i Puri, Odisha. Hans karakteristiske form, med store runde øjne der ser alt, repræsenterer eksistensens absolutte totalitet. Han står med sin bror Baladeva og søster Subhadra og velsigner alle, der kommer til Ham, uanset kaste, tro eller oprindelse.

"The Lord of the universe accepts everyone. There is no restriction. This is the special mercy of Lord Jagannath." "Universets Herre accepterer alle. Der er ingen begrænsning. Dette er den særlige nåde fra Herre Jagannath." — Srila Prabhupada

The Festival of Chariots Vognfestivalen — Ratha-Yatra

One of the world's oldest and most joyous festivals. Lord Jagannath, Baladeva, and Subhadra ride magnificent chariots through the streets, showering blessings upon all who witness or participate. Join us in Copenhagen for this extraordinary celebration. En af verdens ældste og mest glædesfulde festivaler. Herre Jagannath, Baladeva og Subhadra rider på prægtige vogne gennem gaderne og overdænger alle med velsignelser. Deltag i os i København for denne ekstraordinære fejring.
