Seva

Why Give?Hvorfor Give?

In the Bhagavad Gita, Lord Krishna says: "If one offers Me with love and devotion a leaf, a flower, a fruit, or water, I will accept it." Seva — devotional service — is the foundation of bhakti yoga. Your contribution directly supports the Lord's daily worship, the temple's community programmes, and the free distribution of prasadam to all who visit.I Bhagavad Gita siger Herre Krishna: "Hvis nogen tilbyder Mig med kærlighed et blad, en blomst, en frugt eller vand, vil Jeg acceptere det." Seva — hengiven tjeneste — er grundlaget for bhakti yoga. Dit bidrag støtter direkte Herrens daglige tilbedelse og gratis uddeling af prasadam til alle besøgende.
