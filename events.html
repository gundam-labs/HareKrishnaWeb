Janmashtami
Krishna's Appearance DayKrishnas tilsynekomstdag

The most celebrated Vaishnava festival. Midnight marks the exact moment of Lord Krishna's appearance 5,000 years ago. The evening includes kirtan, drama, abhishek ceremony, and prasadam for all.Den mest fejrede vaishnaviske festival. Midnat markerer det nøjagtige øjeblik for Herre Krishnas tilsynekomst for 5.000 år siden. Aftenen inkluderer kirtan, drama, abhishek-ceremoni og prasadam for alle.

Radhashtami
Radharani's Appearance DayRadharanis tilsynekomstdag

The appearance day of Srimati Radharani, the eternal consort of Lord Krishna. Special bhajans, a lecture on Radharani's glories, and beautiful deity decoration.Srimati Radharanis tilsynekomstdag, Herre Krishnas evige ledsager. Særlige bhajaner, foredrag om Radharanis herligheder og smuk gudedekoration.

🕐 10:00 – 20:00  ·  Free entryGratis adgang

Sunday Feast
Weekly · Every SundayUgentlig · Hver søndag

The Sunday programme is the most accessible entry point for newcomers. Kirtan (devotional singing), a class from the Bhagavad Gita, Sandhya Aarti, and a free vegetarian feast.Søndagsprogrammet er det mest tilgængelige indgangspunkt for nytilkomne. Kirtan, en klasse fra Bhagavad Gita, Sandhya Aarti og en gratis vegetarisk fest.
